# My AI Project

Final project for the Building AI course

## Summary

This Building AI course project summarizes my key learnings from the AI certification. I explored AI fundamentals, machine learning, neural networks, data handling, ethics, and practical applications. I also sketched a personal AI idea to apply these concepts in real-world scenarios.

## Background

AI has become a crucial technology across many industries. This project consolidates my understanding of AI concepts and techniques. It addresses the challenge of learning AI in a structured, practical way and documents my progress for future reference.

## Key Learnings

1. **Foundations of AI**
   * Understanding what AI is and its capabilities.
   * Differences between AI, machine learning, and deep learning.
   * Examples of AI in everyday life.

2. **Machine Learning Basics**
   * Supervised vs unsupervised learning.
   * Training, validation, and testing data.
   * Overfitting and underfitting.
   * k-Nearest Neighbors, decision trees, and neural networks.

3. **Neural Networks and Deep Learning**
   * Structure of neurons, layers, weights, and biases.
   * Activation functions: identity, sigmoid, ReLU, softmax.
   * How neural networks learn via backpropagation.

4. **Data Handling**
   * Importance of data quality and preprocessing.
   * Bag-of-words and TF-IDF for text representation.
   * Handling imbalanced datasets.

5. **AI Ethics and Implications**
   * Bias in AI models and fairness.
   * Privacy, transparency, and accountability.
   * Social impacts of AI deployment.

6. **Practical Applications**
   * AI for image recognition, natural language processing, and recommendation systems.
   * Real-world AI project examples and prototyping.

## My AI Idea

* I plan to apply AI to a personal or societal problem, focusing on feasibility and limitations.
* The project will consider datasets, suitable model types, and evaluation metrics.
* The goal is to explore practical implementation and assess potential impact on users.

## Challenges

* Understanding the limits of AI models and ensuring ethical use.
* Handling incomplete or biased data.
* Translating theoretical knowledge into practical solutions.

## Next Steps

* Further research and experimentation with datasets and models.
* Prototyping a small AI solution to test ideas.
* Exploring collaboration opportunities for real-world impact.
